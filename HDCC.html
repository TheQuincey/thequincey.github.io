Heroes Dutch Comic Con

Utrecht

Heroes Dutch Comic Con (HDCC), is currently the largest and most popular Con in the Netherlands. It proudly welcomes more than 55.000 visitors per edition. The event normally lasts 2 days, but for special occasions it also holds a preview night on Friday night, before the usual 2 days.

During these 2 days, many different fandoms come to enjoy the event all-together. Since there are so many fandoms, HDCC’s program offers a various programme, about Comics, games, cosplay, movies, series, superheroes, fantasy / science fiction and anime. There’s always much to do and see, regardless.

There’s tons of entertainment, or decide to become part of the programme itself by attending in cosplay! You can transform yourself into your favorite character and have tons of fun meeting others. Even if you don’t wear a cosplay, you can still fully enjoy watching the other visitors wearing them. Some people even make their own outfits all by themselves!.
