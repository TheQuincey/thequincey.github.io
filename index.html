Heroes Dutch Comic Con

Utrecht

Firstly, we chose to present the most popular con in the Netherlands. Heroes Dutch Comic Con, also known as HDCC for short.

Here you will step into the halls of Utrecht’s jaarbeurs. The activities here will vary from delicious streetfood stands, tons of little shops, and perhaps to even meeting your favorite actor!
