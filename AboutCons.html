If you don’t know what comic cons are yet, then that’s no probem at all! Here I’ll give you a quick summary on to what comic cons are.

Comic Conventions (often known as Comic Cons, or just Cons), are conventions, mostly held in the halls of various Trade Fairs. Of course there are some exceptions, like for example Elfia or Viencon. One is held on a castle’s grounds, while the other is held at a waterpark.

From the start Cons were solely orientated around comic book culture, emphasizing comic books. Here fellow comic book fans would gather to meet one-another or meet the creators of their favorite comic books.

Nowadays the Cons have grown out to be much bigger than what they initially started as. The emphasis still technically lies on comic books, but that’s not very noticable considering there’s thousands of people attending, all with different interests.

A lot of people there don’t even read comic books, but merely attend in a cosplay of their favorite fictional character and go to meetings to have fun with their friends!
