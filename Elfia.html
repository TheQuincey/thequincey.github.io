Elfia

Utrecht/Limburg

Elfia is one of the largest and most popular fantasy events in the Netherlands, attracting thousands of visitors each edition. Held over two days, the festival takes place in the picturesque settings of castles like Castle de Haar in Haarzuilens and Castle Arcen. Elfia is more than just an event; it’s a celebration of creativity, imagination, and the freedom to be whoever or whatever you want to be. During these two days, various fantasy worlds come together to create an enchanting atmosphere. Whether you are a fan of medieval times, wizards, elves, or even steampunk, Elfia offers a diverse program with something for everyone. The event revolves around costumes, fantasy, mythology, and fairy tales, but there’s also room for modern influences such as science fiction and post-apocalyptic themes.

You can fully immerse yourself in the magical atmosphere, whether you come dressed in costume or simply enjoy admiring the breathtaking outfits of other attendees. Many of the costumes are handmade, and there is always something extraordinary to see.

Elfia is a magical experience for all ages, where imagination comes to life, and everyone is welcome to be themselves. Whether you're there to enjoy the creativity of others, become your dream character, or simply soak in the unique atmosphere, Elfia offers an unforgettable adventure!
